Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I book a test?

    Call us at 01140073808 or 7042193187 to book a test.

  • How can I trust your results?

    We follow the pre-requisites and quality standards defined by the government for conducting transplant immunology based assays. Moreover, we DO NOT release any result without cross validation and 100% confidence.

  • What is the relecance of NABL ?

    NABL is an autonomous body that audits a laboratory as per the standard guidelines, which helps the laboratory to minimise errors and structure the routine workflows.

  • What if my test fails?

    In case of a failure at lab end, we repeat the test free of charge for our patiens.

  • Where can I clarify my report or test related doubts?

    Call us anytime at 7042193187.
